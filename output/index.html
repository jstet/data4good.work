What is Data4Good?

Data4Good refers to the use of data and analytics for social and environmental good. This can range from data-driven research and analysis, open data initiatives, data visualization, to the development of data-driven tools and platforms. Data4Good is driven by the belief that data can be a powerful force for good, helping to address pressing challenges instead of solely focusing on maximizing profits.

This website is maintained by a community of contributors. For more information and to contribute, visit the repository where you can find the source code.
